About Ezentials

The gift of ezentials

Our Ezential candles are made of 100% non-toxic soy wax and handpoured with love and intention in San Francisco, CA. Each candle is infused with premium essential oil blends that soothe the soul and contain unique healing qualities.

Give yourself a zen moment. Whether you purchase an Ezential candle for yourself or received one as a gift, lighting our candles sparks an intention of presence and self-care.
